Our Story

My name is Darci and I am so happy to introduce to you "Rio de Maria"...

My bathing suit line is the product of a life-long dream, one that started with a simple beginning in Brazil, my birth country. I love Brazil, my family, and my friends. I had a great job and was attending college there, I had almost everything, yet an important part was missing....my freedom and safety. I decided I had to leave everything behind, it was so scary and challenging, but I held onto God's hand and asked Him to direct me to happiness. Our pieces are manufactured in Cabo Frio, a beautiful beach city outside of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. And Maria, well, she is my Mom, the reason I am here and have become the person I am today! She is the best friend I had in my life but unfortunately she left to go to heaven in 2010; the name is in honor of her.

I have over 17 years of experience in retail and fashion and I am passionate about customer service and People's rights. Please feel free to contact me with any questions or concerns you may have, we will be happy to help you!
